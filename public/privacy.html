Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2025

Your privacy matters. Here's what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Email: Only if you voluntarily join the mailing list
  • Name: Collected in-app for personalization only (e.g., “Hi Alex”)
  • Test & Streak Data: Saved to analyze learning patterns and improve the app

How we use your data

  • Email is used for occasional updates about Tonely
  • Test data is used for research and feature development

What we don’t collect

  • No payment information
  • No microphone, camera, or location tracking
  • No personal identifiers beyond your chosen name

Data storage

All data is securely stored in a cloud database with access restricted to the Tonely team. We never sell or share your data.

Your rights

You can request your data to be deleted at any time by emailing us at lucas.pauker@gmail.com.
